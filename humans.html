Meet the Team

Humans of

Portraits of, and short conversations with, just some of the thousands of people across the world who make the Euclid mission possible.

Humans of Euclid is a photoblog and series of interviews that explores the different perspectives and experiences of people involved in an international mission. Whether it's engineering, business, web development, creative writing, accountancy, art, scientific research, data analysis, computing or education, missions like Euclid offer a wider range of career opportunities than you may realise. Euclid's beautiful images and groundbreaking scientific results of the cosmos are only possible because these humans work together, collaborating beyond borders.
